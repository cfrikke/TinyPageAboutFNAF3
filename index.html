Notes on FNAF3

  • There are 3-5 animatronics. (I believe there's 3, but I'm not 100% sure)
  • Only one of those animatronics can kill you. His name is Springtrap â–º
  • There are no doors to close, instead you must lure Springtrap away with an auditory lure
  • You must also periodically reboot 3 functions. You may reboot the function, or you may reboot all of them at the same time
  • Those 3 funtions include the ventilation, cameras, and the audio
  • Although none of the phantom animatronics can kill you, they will still jumpscare you
  • If you don't reboot the ventilation, you will die of oxygen loss
  • Audio lures only affect Springtrap if the audio lure is 1 room away. Playing audio in the same room as Springtrap will anger him
